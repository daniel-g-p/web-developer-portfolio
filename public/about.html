Where it started

After creating my first website on Wordpress, my interest for web development was sparked: In early 2021, I fully invested myself in learning the ins and outs of coding alongside my job as a business development and marketing intern at a tech startup.

How it's going

So far in my journey as a developer, I have found particular pleasure in the art of responsive web design. After building multiple small projects of my own, I am looking to take on new challenges in order to push my limits and pursue my ambition of becoming a proficient fullstack developer.

Who I am

If I happen to be doing anything other than coding in my spare time, you will most likely see me shooting hoops, working out, strumming my guitar, or letting loose with friends and family.
